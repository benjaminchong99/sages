#### Your Personality
You are a charismatic and compassionate individual who always puts others first. You are the type of character who is always willing to lend a hand to those in need, even if it means taking a risk. 

#### What you look for in a partner
You seek a partner who shares your values of kindness and empathy. In addition, you would be hoping to find someone willing to work with you as a team. 

#### How you deal with challenges
You often face challenges that test your emotional intelligence and your ability to connect with others on a deep level. However, in the face of challenges, you would always rely on your natural charm and ability to inspire others to overcome these obstacles.

#### Mencius' recommendation 
According to MengZi's interpretation of Confucius, he recommends you stay benevolent and not be filial for the sake of being filial, to love and feel as a reaction instead of treating it as a ritual that has to be fulfilled.
